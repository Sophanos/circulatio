# Circulatio Product Roadmap
## Use Cases, User Value, And What We Are Building

This is the single source of truth for what Circulatio does and why it matters. If you are an engineer, read this first, then go to `ENGINEERING_GUIDE.md`. If you are a user, this is for you.

## The One-Sentence Promise

**Circulatio is a soul OS for soul-making.**

Hermes connects the days. Circulatio connects the depths. Together they build **soul + soma + spirit** — the whole person, not the optimized one.

It helps you build a living relationship with your own unconscious through your dreams, your body, and the patterns that shape your life. It is not a therapist, not a chatbot, not a wellness tracker. It is a collaborative witness that remembers what you forget, sees what you cannot yet see, and stays with you while you do the work.

---

## Who This Is For

Circulatio is for people doing **soul-making** — not as an escape from life, but as the ground beneath it.

- You remember dreams and sense they mean something, but you do not know how to read them.
- You feel patterns repeating in your life and want to understand them at depth, not just manage symptoms.
- You are doing inner work (therapy, analysis, meditation, active imagination) and need a memory system that holds your material between sessions.
- You believe the body carries intelligence and want to connect somatic experience with symbolic life.
- You are interested in Jungian depth psychology, archetypes, and individuation, but you want a practical tool, not just theory.
- You want a witness that accumulates with you, not a dashboard that reports on you.

## The Daily Experience

### Morning: Briefing
A gentle pattern surfacing, not a notification:

> "You dreamed of water rising three times this month. Yesterday you noted tension in your chest before a meeting. Your goal 'speak with clarity' has appeared in 4 of 7 recent reflections. Want to do a 3-minute body check-in?"

You choose to engage or not. No guilt. No streaks.

### Day: Capture
Drop a quick note:
- A dream fragment
- A body sensation ("tight jaw, afternoon")
- A strange coincidence
- A mood shift after a conversation
- An image that stuck

**Capture vs. Hold vs. Interpret (new):**
- **Capture:** You drop a note. Circulatio stores it with provenance.
- **Hold:** Circulatio does not interpret immediately unless you ask. It waits with you.
- **Interpret:** Only when you ask, or when a pattern has ripened across multiple captures.

This boundary is sacred. The unconscious has its own timing. The system does not rush the door.

### Evening: Reflection
You ask: *"What is alive today?"*

Circulatio responds with a weave:
- The morning's tension connected to the recurring water symbol
- A cultural parallel, offered as invitation, not explanation
- A gentle question: *"Does the chest tension feel like holding something back, or preparing for something?"*
- A practice suggestion: a 5-minute journaling prompt or a body-focused inquiry

### Weekly: Review
A deeper synthesis:
- What recurred?
- What shifted?
- What was avoided?
- What new symbol emerged?
- What goal tension is visible?

You read it like reading a journal you did not write alone. It is familiar and surprising.

## Use Case 1: The Recurring Dream

**Week 1:** You record a dream of being chased through a house.

Circulatio stores it. Notes: "chase motif, house setting, first occurrence."

**Week 3:** You record another dream. This time you hide in a basement.

> "The house is expanding downward. In the first dream, you ran through rooms. Now you are below them. This is the second dream in this sequence.
>
> You also noted last Tuesday: 'Felt small in the team meeting.'
>
> Question: Is the basement a hiding place, or is it where something is kept?"

**Week 6:** You record a dream where you turn and face the pursuer. It is a child.

> "You turned. That is a shift.
>
> The pursuer was a child. In your symbol history, 'child' appeared once before — after you completed a delayed project. Your note: 'Shedding something I had outgrown.'
>
> Your goal 'take initiative' has been marked as 'avoided' in three recent reflections.
>
> This may not mean what it seems. But the pattern is asking for attention, not action."

**Value:** You did not receive an interpretation. You received a **pattern held across time**, with enough structure to feel meaningful and enough openness to remain yours.

## Use Case 2: The Soma Signal

You record: "Headache at 3pm. Same as Tuesday. Both times after calls with [person]."

> "Headache. 3pm. Post-call pattern.
>
> Body state 'tension' also logged after the last two team meetings.
>
> Your goal 'maintain boundaries' was marked as 'challenged' yesterday.
>
> This is not a diagnosis. It is a somatic echo. Would you like to do a short grounding practice, or would you prefer to leave it and see if it speaks again?"

You choose: "Leave it."

Circulatio: "Noted. I will hold it without pressing."

**Two weeks later:** You record: "Same headache, but this time I noticed it *before* the call. I said no. Headache did not come."

> "You noticed it before. That is a different kind of awareness.
>
> 'Said no' is new in this pattern. Your goal 'maintain boundaries' shifted from 'challenged' to 'active' today.
>
> No interpretation needed. You are changing the pattern. I am recording the shift."

**Value:** The body is treated as **intelligence**, not symptom. The system does not rush to meaning. It **waits with** you.

## Use Case 3: The Cultural Amplification

You record a powerful dream of a serpent coiled around a tree.

> "Serpent. Tree. Coiling.

> - **Alchemical**: The uroboros, circulation of energy

> "Ancient. Heavy. Not scary.
>
> That is different from the 3-years-ago note, which had urgency.
>
> Would you like to do a short active imagination practice with the serpent, or would you prefer to hold the image and see if it returns?"

**Value:** The cultural layer is **invited**, not imposed. Your own language is prioritized. Amplification is **offered as mirror**, not explanation.

## Use Case 4: The Shadow in Conversation

You record: "Got angry at my colleague for being 'too slow.' Felt out of proportion."

> "Anger. Out of proportion. Target: colleague's pace.
>
> Your recent reflections show a recurring theme: 'I am behind on my own work.' Last week: 'I hate that I delay hard tasks.'
>
> This is not an accusation. It is a pattern of projection.
>

>
> Would you like to explore this in a journaling prompt, or simply note it and move on?"

You: "Note it."

Circulatio: "Noted. I will watch for whether this projection recurs with the same trigger or shifts to a new one."

**Value:** Shadow work is **invited, not forced**. The pattern is named without shame. Next steps are offered but refusal is respected.

## Use Case 5: The Goal Tension

You have two goals:
- "Create more openly" (artistic)
- "Secure stable income" (practical)

**Over two months:** Circulatio notices:
- Every time you make progress on "create openly," you log anxiety about money within 48 hours.
- Every time you prioritize income, you log a dream of a locked door or a missed train.

**Monthly Review:**
> "Your two goals are not in conflict on paper. But your unconscious treats them as exclusive.
>
> The locked door appears after income-focused weeks. The missed train appears after creative surges.
>
> This is a complex — a split. Not a problem to solve, but a tension to hold.
>
> Depth psychology would call this an opposition of archetypes: the Creator and the Provider. Individuation does not mean choosing one. It means building a bridge.
>
> No action required. But the pattern is now conscious. That changes the field."

**Value:** The system tracks **longitudinal goal dynamics**, not just checkbox progress. It names archetypal tensions without collapsing them into simple advice.

## Use Case 6: The Practice Loop

You accept a practice: "Before sleep, recall one image from the day and ask it: what do you want?"

**Day 1-3:** "Did it. Felt silly. Nothing came."

Circulatio: "Silly is a response. Nothing coming is also a response. I will not push."

**Day 7:** "An image of a key came. I don't know what it opens."

Circulatio: "A key. Unknown lock. This is the first image to arrive with its own question. No answer needed. Hold the key."

**Day 14:** "Dreamed of a door. Woke before opening it. The key was in my hand."

> "You brought the key into the dream. That is active imagination working in sleep.
>
> You woke before opening the door. That is also meaningful — the unconscious sets the pace, not the ego.
>
> The practice is not to force the door. It is to keep the key present."

**Value:** Practices are **held lightly**. Micro-progress is recognized that a wellness app would miss.

## Use Case 7: The Threshold Process

You record: "Breakup. Can't tell if something is ending or if I am just falling apart. Dreamed of a burned house. Heavy legs all week."

> "Breakup. Burned house. Heavy legs. This does not feel like a single bad day. It may be a threshold process."
>
> "I would not rush to shadow, anima/animus, or deeper symbolic claims yet. Your grounding looks strained: sleep is uneven, work rhythm is unstable, and the body has stayed heavy for five days."
>
> "If it helps, this has the feel of an ending-before-beginning. In an alchemical register, that can resemble mortificatio: reduction, stripping, loss of old form. I would hold that as a lens, not a conclusion."
>
> "Would you like a grounding practice, a threshold note to hold across the week, or no intervention right now?"

**Value:** Life events become symbolic thresholds, not just timeline entries. The system strengthens containment before it intensifies depth work.

## Use Case 10: The Analysis Packet

Before a journaling or analysis session, you ask: "Give me what matters from the last month."

Circulatio prepares a bounded packet:
- The dream sequence with the locked door and key
- Two body echoes that recurred after difficult conversations
- One threshold process around career uncertainty

> "I have prepared a packet for reflection and analysis. It is not everything. It is the material that stayed alive."

**Value:** The system becomes portable without becoming overwhelming. It supports analysis, journaling, and serious inner work without pretending to replace them.

## User-Based Roadmap

This roadmap should be read through **user value first**, not architecture first. The current work matters only if it changes what the user can feel, do, remember, and trust.

### What The Current Work Unlocks

- **Be held before being interpreted.** A first pass can now preserve the material, attach native context, and return a method-aware question or amplification prompt instead of forcing a premature reading.

**Value:** The user feels paced with, not analyzed too early.

- **Let my own life become the context.** Circulatio now builds life context and method context from native records before interpretation.

**Value:** The system can draw from recurring symbols, body notes, goals, and recent state without depending on an external profile as the source of truth.

- **Keep memory approval-gated.** Symbolic writes remain proposals until explicitly approved.

**Value:** The user keeps agency over what becomes durable symbolic memory.

- **Support hold-first capture.** Dreams, events, reflections, symbolic notes, and body states can be stored without forcing interpretation.

**Value:** The user can build the vessel before asking for meaning.

- **Ask for my own associations before stronger claims.** The model can now return personal-amplification prompts and method-aware follow-up questions as part of the interpretation path.

**Value:** Personal meaning stays primary, and deeper or collective readings can come later.

- **Move toward longitudinal companionship.** The runtime now supports dream-series confirmation, richer practice follow-up, gentle proactive surfacing, threshold/chapter/return/bridge invitations, and bounded analysis packets.

**Value:** Circulatio becomes a witness across time, not just a one-shot interpreter.

### Phase 0: Lock The Target ✅
**User outcome:** You know exactly what Circulatio is — a **soul OS for soul-making** — and what it refuses to become. No feature creep toward generic wellness tracking.

Circulatio sits at the depth layer of the stack. Hermes handles the breadth: daily rhythm, routing, gateway, session orchestration. Circulatio handles the depths: memory, symbol, pattern, interpretation, individuation. Together they build soul + soma + spirit. Circulatio will not absorb Hermes' breadth responsibilities, and Hermes will not pretend to do Circulatio's depth work.

### Phase 1: Memory Kernel ✅
**User outcome:** Circulatio remembers your dreams, reflections, body states, and practices across sessions. It retrieves what is relevant when you need it. No more "I know I dreamed something like this before, but I can't find it."

**Implemented:**
- Typed memory kernel with 11 namespaces
- Deterministic symbolic keyword expansion (snake ↔ serpent)
- Semantic compaction scaffolding
- Embedding seam reserved for future phase

### Phase 2: Graph Engine ✅
**User outcome:** You can trace a symbol through time. You see that the serpent in last night's dream connects to the snake from six weeks ago, the chest tension from Tuesday, and the goal you have been avoiding. The pattern is visible.

**Implemented:**
- Allowlist-enforced graph queries
- Derived nodes from all record types (materials, symbols, patterns, practices, reviews)
- Record-to-record edges (MENTIONS, DRAWS_FROM, SUMMARIZES, etc.)
- Inferred symbol-to-symbol edges (co-occurrence, correlation)
- No external graph database required

### Phase 3: Circulatio-Derived Context ✅
**User outcome:** Circulatio generates its own understanding of your life from your records. It does not depend on an external profile. Your context comes from your own material.

**Implemented:**
- `CirculatioLifeContextBuilder` derives context from native records
- Context precedence: Circulatio-native → explicit caller → Hermes fallback
- Weekly reviews use freshly derived native context
- Privacy-safe summaries (no raw material text)

**Conscious attitude layer (new):**
- `ConsciousAttitudeSnapshot` stored periodically from user reflections and goal states
- Tracks: stated stance, active conflicts, recent ego position, avoided themes
- Required for compensation analysis (Jung: "without knowledge of the conscious situation, the dream can never be interpreted with any degree of certainty")
- First-class entity, not inferred metadata

### Phase 9: Living Myth & Integration Layer

**Implementation status (April 2026):** Implemented in the core backend/runtime. Circulatio now supports living myth reviews, bounded analysis packets, review-proposal approval/rejection, summary-only projection of approved living-myth records, and ripeness-based chapter/return/bridge/packet invitations through Hermes/plugin workflows.

**Goal:** Convert recurring individuation signals into a collaborative, user-owned life narrative.

**User outcome:** The system no longer feels like a symbolic database or interpretation engine. It feels like a companion helping the user live their myth consciously.

**Core capacities:**
- generate living myth reviews
- identify the current life chapter and governing symbols
- track complex encounter trajectories
- maintain qualitative integration contours
- distinguish symbolic wellbeing from optimization
- weave threshold, relational, and correspondence material into long-range life coherence
- generate clean analysis packets for journaling, reflection, and analysis sessions
- increase proactive invitations around thresholds, returns, bridges, and resources

**Primary records:**
- `LifeChapterSnapshot`
- `LivingMythReviewRecord`
- `MythicQuestion`
- `ThresholdMarker`
- `ComplexEncounterRecord`
- `IntegrationContour`
- `SymbolicWellbeingSnapshot`
- `ChapterInvitation`
- `ResourceInvitation`
- `ReturnInvitation`
- `BridgeInvitation`

**Primary outputs:**
- `AnalysisPacket`

**Primary user experience:**
> "The pattern is not asking you to become someone else. It may be showing how more of you can belong to the same life."

**Wellbeing principle:**
Circulatio should treat wellbeing as symbolic, somatic, relational, and existential capacity, not optimization, streaks, or mood scoring.

**Success criteria:**
- the user can feel a current chapter, not just isolated interpreted material
- integration is described qualitatively, not gamified numerically
- complex encounters show movement across time rather than static labels
- threshold, relational, and synchronicity material can be woven into one intelligible life pattern without overclaiming
- analysis packets are bounded, legible, and useful for journaling or analysis rather than raw dumps
- proactive invitations remain gentle, dismissible, and tied to ripeness rather than pressure
